Here's a little more detail

Getting started with GGMC is quick and easy. Download the app, verify your identity with a photo ID and a selfie, and you'll have your account ready in minutes. Your virtual card is available immediately — the physical card arrives in a few days.
The GGMC app is available on both the App Store and Google Play. Search for "GGMC" and download it for free. You'll need a smartphone running iOS 15 or later, or Android 10 or later.
You can open additional current accounts directly in the app at no cost. These extra accounts are great for budgeting, separating spending, or saving for specific goals. Each account comes with its own sort code and account number.
