Introducing GGMC Protect

Think of our packaged insurance cover as a way to protect yourself, both at home and abroad. You'll get worldwide travel insurance, mobile phone insurance and UK breakdown cover, all for just £12.50 a month.

Limitations, exclusions, excess and T&Cs apply.

What's in the package?

Your travels, your phone and your UK road trips – all three covered for less than you might expect. We've partnered with leading providers to give you a trio of insurance benefits, so you can live life and travel with added peace of mind.

Worldwide travel insurance

You'll get multi-trip cover for you, your partner and up to 4 children, so you can travel with confidence knowing you've got a solid safety net.

Our partner specialises in leisure travel coverage, leveraging expertise in medical screening, claims processing, and 24/7 emergency medical assistance.

  • Emergency medical & dental expenses up to £10 million
  • Cancellation cover up to £5,000 per person
  • Baggage & personal belongings up to £2,000
  • Travel delay cover
  • Personal liability up to £2 million
  • 24/7 emergency assistance line
  • Trips over 31 days without extension
  • Pre-existing conditions not declared
  • Extreme sports & hazardous activities
  • Travel against government advice

Mobile phone insurance

We know life's most precious memories are captured and stored on your phone – so keeping it safe matters. Our worldwide cover protects you against loss, theft, damage and technical faults, wherever life takes you.

Our partner is a global provider of mobile device protection.

  • Accidental damage & cracked screens
  • Loss & theft worldwide
  • Mechanical & electrical breakdown
  • Liquid damage
  • Unauthorised usage up to £1,000
  • Up to 3 devices covered per account
  • Cosmetic damage that doesn't affect function
  • Phones worth more than £2,000
  • Pre-existing faults or damage
  • Accessories & SIM cards

Breakdown cover

Reach the places you need to go and the people you want to see. With UK-wide breakdown cover from our trusted partner, you're covered as the driver or passenger of any eligible vehicle.

Our partner is a UK market leader in roadside assistance and breakdown recovery.

  • Roadside assistance & repair
  • Home start if your vehicle won't start
  • National recovery to a destination of choice
  • Onward travel if repair isn't possible
  • Cover as driver or passenger in any eligible vehicle
  • Vehicles over 3.5 tonnes
  • Breakdowns outside the UK
  • Fuel, keys, or tyre replacements
  • Commercial & hire vehicles

Who can get GGMC Protect?

  • Be aged between 18 and 69
  • Have a GGMC current account
  • Be registered with a GP in the UK
  • Have been a UK tax resident for at least six months
  • Have valid proof of ownership for any mobile phones you wish to cover – worth no more than £2,000 each
